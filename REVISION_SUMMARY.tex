\subsection{Issue 5: Fix Moran's I p-value}

\textbf{Before:} Invalid approximation: $p = 2(1 - |z|/(1+|z|)) = 1.227$ {\color{red}❌}

\textbf{After:} Correct two-tailed normal CDF
\begin{verbatim}

\end{verbatim}

\textbf{Result:} $p \approx 0.529$ (valid probability)

\textcolor{green}{$\checkmark$ Mathematically correct}
